The lower F1 score obtained in comparison to the other two prediction methods can be somewhat attributed to the architectural complexity. On the other hand, the average F1 scores for the Point Cloud and Mesh prediction models were much higher. Particularly because predicting connectivity information is not necessary for the Point Cloud prediction task, it is significantly simpler. Mesh prediction, on the other hand, calls for capturing both the geometric shape and the connectivity information, making it a more complex task that, while difficult, ultimately pays off in higher F1 scores.
